Frequently Asked Questions

What is engineering team augmentation?

Engineering team augmentation is a model where you hire dedicated developers who integrate directly into your existing team. Unlike traditional outsourcing, augmented engineers join your Slack, attend your standups, and commit to your repos. At Sifr Ventures, we use a build-to-staff approach: we recruit engineers specifically for your stack and embed them as full members of your team.

How quickly can you ramp up a dedicated development team?

We can ramp up a dedicated development team in 2 to 4 weeks. Our established hiring pipeline lets us move from role definition to an onboarded, productive engineer in that timeframe. We started with one engineer for our first client and grew to a complete cross-functional team using this process.

What's the difference between staff augmentation and outsourcing?

Staff augmentation embeds engineers into your team. They work your hours, use your tools, and report to your leads. Outsourcing hands off a project or module to an external team that manages itself. With augmentation, you keep full control of priorities, architecture, and code quality. We focus exclusively on augmentation because embedded teams produce better outcomes than handoffs.

Do your developers integrate with our existing workflows?

Yes. Our developers join your existing workflows from day one. That means your Slack channels, your daily standups, your code review process, and your CI/CD pipeline. We don't run a parallel process. The goal is that after the first week, your augmented engineers are indistinguishable from your in-house team in terms of process and communication.
